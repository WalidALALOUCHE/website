Global Debate Summit 2025: Registrations Now Open

Sarah Johnson
May 15, 2024 8 min read

The Global Debate Summit 2025, the most anticipated debate event of the year, is now accepting registrations. Scheduled to take place in Geneva from July 10-15, 2025, this premier event brings together the world's most talented debaters, esteemed jurors, and influential thought leaders for five days of intellectual discourse, skill development, and global networking.

What to Expect at the Summit

The Global Debate Summit is more than just a competition—it's a comprehensive experience designed to nurture debate talent and foster meaningful connections. This year's summit will feature:

  • Championship Debates: Witness riveting debates on pressing global issues by champions from different debate formats and traditions.
  • Masterclasses: Learn from world-renowned debate coaches and public speaking experts through intensive workshops.
  • Networking Events: Connect with fellow debaters, coaches, and professionals from over 40 countries.
  • Cultural Exchanges: Participate in cultural activities that celebrate the diversity of the global debate community.
  • Innovation Forum: Explore the future of debate education and competition in our rapidly changing world.
"The Global Debate Summit represents the pinnacle of debate excellence. It's where traditions meet innovation, and where the global debate community comes together to celebrate the power of reasoned argument and effective communication." — Michael Chen, Head of Jury Affairs

This Year's Theme: "Bridging Divides Through Dialogue"

In an era marked by polarization and complex global challenges, the 2025 Summit will focus on how debate can be a tool for bridging divides and finding common ground. Participants will explore how the principles of structured argument, active listening, and evidence-based reasoning can contribute to more productive discourse on contentious issues.

A debate session from the 2023 Global Debate Summit

Registration Details

Participation in the Summit is open to debaters of all levels, from beginners to champions. However, spaces are limited, and the application process is competitive. Here's what you need to know:

Participant Categories

  • Competitor: For active debaters who wish to participate in the championship tournaments.
  • Delegate: For those who want to attend workshops, networking events, and observe the competitions.
  • Educator: A special track for debate coaches, teachers, and program directors.

Important Dates

  • Early Bird Registration: May 15 - July 31, 2024
  • Regular Registration: August 1 - December 15, 2024
  • Late Registration: December 16, 2024 - March 1, 2025
  • Application Review Period: March 2-31, 2025
  • Final Selections Announced: April 15, 2025

Ready to join the global debate community?

Early bird registrants receive a 25% discount on registration fees and priority access to limited-capacity masterclasses.

Register Now

Scholarship Opportunities

All-Star Debate is committed to ensuring that financial constraints do not prevent talented debaters from participating in this transformative experience. A limited number of full and partial scholarships are available for applicants demonstrating exceptional potential and financial need.

Scholarship applications must be submitted alongside your registration by September 30, 2024, and require additional documentation including a personal statement and letter of recommendation.

Accommodation and Travel

The Summit will take place at the International Conference Center Geneva (CICG). Special rates have been negotiated with partner hotels ranging from budget-friendly to luxury options. Registered participants will receive a detailed information packet with accommodation options and travel tips.

International Conference Center Geneva, venue for the 2025 Summit

Final Thoughts

The Global Debate Summit 2025 promises to be a landmark event in the debate calendar. Whether you're looking to compete against the best, learn from world-class educators, or expand your professional network, this summit offers unparalleled opportunities for growth and connection.

For any questions regarding registration, accommodations, or the Summit program, please contact our team at summit@allstardebate.org.

We look forward to welcoming you to Geneva in July 2025!
