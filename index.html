About Us

All-Star Debate is a national debate championship that brings together the best university talents. Beyond competition: a forum for exchange, excellence, and national youth dialogue.

Our mission is to empower youth voice, promote critical thinking, and elevate debate culture nationally.

Word from the Co-founders

"Our vision is to create a space where young people can freely express their ideas and learn to debate constructively. Debate is a powerful tool for developing critical thinking and self-confidence."

- Walid Alalouche, Co-founder

"Through All-Star Debate, we aspire to instill a culture of respectful dialogue and exchange of ideas. We firmly believe that debate can be a vector of positive change in our society."

- Marouane Benkerroum, Co-founder

Our Mission

Education

Train young talent in debate techniques, public speaking, and constructing logical, well-structured arguments.

Dialogue

Promote a culture of respectful dialogue and exchange of ideas among youth from diverse backgrounds and opinions.

Community

Create a dynamic community of debaters throughout Morocco and encourage intercultural exchanges.
