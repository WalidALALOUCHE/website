Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate debate enthusiasts, All-Star Debate began as a small local competition in Geneva. What started as a modest gathering of 12 debaters has now evolved into one of the most prestigious international debate platforms.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to fostering eloquence, critical thinking, and meaningful dialogue across cultures and backgrounds. Each year, we have expanded our reach, welcoming participants from more countries and diverse walks of life.

Today, All-Star Debate stands as a beacon for intellectual discourse, having hosted over 500 debaters from 35 countries across 5 continents. Our alumni have gone on to become influential leaders, thinkers, and communicators in their respective fields.
