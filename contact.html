Frequently Asked Questions

How can I participate in the All-Star Debate?

To participate as a debater, you need to apply through our Debaters page. Fill out the application form with your details and experience. If you want to be part of the jury, you can make a reservation through our Jury Reservation page.

What are the requirements to be a debater?

Debaters should have previous experience in formal debates or public speaking. They should be able to articulate ideas clearly, research topics thoroughly, and respond to arguments constructively. Proficiency in English is required, and knowledge of French is beneficial but not mandatory.

How can I purchase tickets for the debate events?

Tickets for our debate events can be purchased through our Reservation page. Simply select the event you wish to attend, choose your preferred seating, and complete the checkout process. Early bookings often qualify for discounted rates.

Are there opportunities for sponsorship?

Yes, we offer various sponsorship packages for companies and organizations interested in supporting our debates. Sponsorship provides visibility to a highly engaged audience and demonstrates your commitment to intellectual discourse. Please contact our sponsorship team at sponsors@allstardebate.com for details.

Can I suggest topics for future debates?

Absolutely! We welcome topic suggestions from our community. You can submit your ideas through the contact form on this page. Our committee reviews all suggestions and selects topics based on relevance, potential for meaningful discourse, and audience interest.
